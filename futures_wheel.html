Futures Wheel Workshop

Introduction and warm-up task (10 min): We will start with team introductions and a quick warm-up exercise to get everyone thinking about AI impacts.

Brief tutorial (5 min): We will show you how to build a Futures Wheel step by step.

Build the wheel (45 min): Working in teams, you will brainstorm positive and negative impacts of an AI use across three rings of consequences.

Analyse and ideate (35 min): Your team will then identify key benefits, risks, and mitigations for this AI use from your completed wheel.

Workshop conclusion (5 min): We will wrap up with key takeaways and reflections on the process.

Complete Post-study Questionnaire (10 min): Finally, you will each complete a post-study questionnaire on your experience with this workshop and for identifying AI benefits, risks and mitigations.

Central Topic

Nominate a Team Coordinator

First, please nominate one person in your team to be the Coordinator. This person will be responsible for adding your team's ideas to the Miro board. The rest of your team will be 'Viewers'.

A key principle of the Futures Wheel is ensuring that all impacts are plausible. Before the Coordinator adds an impact to the board, your team must first discuss it and agree that it is a plausible impact.
