Empathy Mapping Workshop

Introduction and warm-up task (10 min): We will start with team introductions and a quick warm-up exercise to get everyone thinking about different user perspectives.

Brief tutorial (5 min): We will show you how empathy mapping works step by step.

Select personas (5 min): Choose two personas: one similar to your team and one very different.

Create empathy maps (40 min): Working in teams, you will map what each persona sees, hears, says, does, thinks, and feels.

Analyse and ideate (30 min): Finally, you will identify key benefits, risks, and mitigations based on your empathy maps.

Workshop conclusion (5 min): We will wrap up with key takeaways and reflections on the process.

Complete Post-study Questionnaire (10 min): Finally, you will each complete a post-study questionnaire on your experience with this workshop and for identifying AI benefits, risks and mitigations.

How Empathy Mapping Works

Nominate a Team Coordinator

First, please nominate one person in your team to be the Coordinator. This person will be responsible for adding your team's ideas to the Miro board. The rest of your team will be 'Viewers'.

A key principle of Empathy Mapping is ensuring that all insights are plausible. Before the Coordinator adds an idea to the board, your team must first discuss it and agree that it is a plausible insight.
